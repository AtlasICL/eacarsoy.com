Music

As a pianist and music theory enthusiast, performing music live has been a big part of my life. From hosting shows to leading my own band (Les petites Mouettes), performing has helped me grow as a musician and as a person. Below you will find some highlights from my musical journey.

Tabernacle Show - Les petites Mouettes

Watch part of our performance on YouTube

We had the great pleasure of performing to an audience of over 1000 people at the Tabernacle in Notting Hill. Built in 1887 as a church, it is today a Grade II listed building. It was a great experience to perform in such a beautiful and historic venue.

Hosting the LFCG Talent Show - 2022 edition

Watch on YouTube

This was my first time hosting the LFCG Talent Show. I love public speaking and performing, and being able to work closely with so many talented students along with the prestigious jury members was a great experience.
